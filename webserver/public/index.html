ABOUT THE MOINTOR

Our system will have a webcam to stream video and a microphone to stream audio. In addition, our system will notify users based on certain events (i.e. spike in audio) and have a speaker to playback saved audio (i.e for parents to comfort thir baby). Some additional features are remte control of the system, including turning the systemm on/off, moving the camera (could be automatic based on motion sensor), controlling the audio volume, zooming in/out of video, etc.
